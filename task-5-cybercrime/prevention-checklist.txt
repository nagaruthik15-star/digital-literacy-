 Stay Safe Online – Checklist (for College Students in India)
1) Use strong, unique passwords for each account (avoid using the same password everywhere).
2) Enable 2-Factor Authentication (2FA) on important apps like email, banking, and social media.
3) Never click on unknown links received via SMS, email, or apps like WhatsApp.
4) Think before you share personal information (OTP, passwords, college ID, address).
5) Never share your UPI PIN with anyone—even if they claim to be from a bank or support team.
6) Do not approve unknown payment requests on apps like Google Pay or PhonePe.
7) Use only secure Wi-Fi networks; avoid logging into sensitive accounts on public Wi-Fi.
8) Install apps only from official stores (Play Store/App Store) and check reviews before downloading.
9) Regularly update your device and apps to stay protected from security vulnerabilities.
10) Report cybercrime immediately on the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call Helpline 1930.
